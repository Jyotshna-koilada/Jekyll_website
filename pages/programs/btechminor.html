B.Tech Minor in AI & ML Curriculum (12 credits)



Students have to finish a total of 12 credits, with at least one course from each of the categories (rows) below. If a student has already completed some of these categories as part of the regular B.Tech Program, the student should take an equivalent number of elective credits to compensate.
