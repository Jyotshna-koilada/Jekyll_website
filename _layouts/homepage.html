The Department of AI @ IIT Hyderabad's mission is to produce students with a sound understanding of the fundamentals of the theory and practise of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The mission is also to enable students to become leaders in the industry and academia nationally and internationally. Finally, the mission is to meet the pressing demands of the nation in the areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning



Professional Program in AI and Emerging Technologies

A 5-week full-time Professional Program in Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies, jointly offered by IIT Hyderabad and TalentSprint, and delivered on IIT Hyderabad's Campus during the summer of 2019. The Program is best suited for Young Professionals and College Graduates seeking to build world-class expertise in AI and emerging technologies, and obtain professional certification from IIT Hyderabad. Faculty recruitment in AI

The Department of Artificial Intelligence (AI) at IIT Hyderabad invites applications for faculty positions from outstanding candidates with research expertise in any area of AI, including the intersection of AI with other disciplines. We welcome applications from candidates with a PhD in the area of AI and its applications to various fields. They should have a strong academic background in any area of science and engineering, but require an outstanding publication record in any area of AI and its applications.
